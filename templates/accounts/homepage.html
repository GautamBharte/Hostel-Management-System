Karmaveer Boys' Hostel

Every room of this three-storeyed hostel is equipped with two tables, two chairs, two beds and one cupboard which holds quite adequete for all the belongings of the students. The toilets and bathrooms are well maintainf along with the 24/7 supply of electricity and water. The hostel is surrounded with greenery which ensures good ventilation to the rooms.
